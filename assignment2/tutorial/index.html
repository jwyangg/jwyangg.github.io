  • Paper Windmill
  • Tutorial
  1. Gather all materials
  2. Cut 12.5cm square of foam
  3. Fold it in half to make a triangle and press down
  4. Turn around and repeat step 3
  5. Make a hole in the middle, where the creases meet
  6. Make some cuts and add more holes
  7. Add glue under the holes
  8. Bring the flaps down so the holes meet and stick down, repeat for each side
  9. Press down hard and create an imprint using a glue stick and cut out
  10. Make a hole in the cut out shape
  11. Take a pipe cleaner and wrap it around the straw, assemble the washers and through the windmill
  12. Cut the pipe cleaner, wrap it around
